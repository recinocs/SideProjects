Welcome to Average Collector

Search through the baseball card collection of a single, average collector

I've been collecting baseball cards since the age of 11, after getting a blaster box of Topps Opening Day from my Dad. I've always had collecting in my life, be it Yugioh cards, baseball cards, or the need to complete all parts of a video game. There is just an amazing feeling of getting some new card to add to my collection that I didn't have previously. This application was made to show just some of the cards that I've collected throughout my (short) time collecting baseball cards, as an average collector

Any card in this application I'm open to trading for anything that will go into my personal collection. In addition, if there are any cards/teams that may help your personal collection, please feel free to mention it in a trade request and I'll see if I have anything that you might be interested that isn't on AverageCollector


And now for a quick Q&A!

Q. You mentioned your personal collection. What cards are you looking for?
A. Player-wise, I collect Bryce Harper and Trea Turner. Team-wise, it's definitely the Nationals and then older Yankees players.
Q. Would you be willing to sell any cards shown here?
A. No.
Q. What sets do you typically look to buy?
A. Either rookie/prospect sets, like Bowman or Pro Debut or high-end sets like Triple Threads and Museum. The appeal of a bunch of base cards has dwindled a bit as I've gotten older.
